Travel Services

Flight Booking:

Allows users to search and book flights to their desired destinations. Provides information on flight schedules, availability, prices, and airlines.

Hotel Booking:

Enables users to search for and reserve accommodations (e.g., hotels, resorts, hostels) at their travel destinations. Offers details on available rooms, amenities, prices, and guest reviews.

Car Rental:

Allows travelers to rent vehicles (cars, vans, etc.) for transportation at their destination. Provides options for selecting vehicle types, rental durations, and pickup/drop-off locations.

Tour Packages:

Offers pre-planned travel packages including flights, accommodations, and tours or activities. Provides a curated travel experience, often themed around specific destinations or activities.

Travel Insurance:

Provides options for purchasing travel insurance to cover unexpected events during the trip, such as trip cancellations, medical emergencies, or lost luggage.
